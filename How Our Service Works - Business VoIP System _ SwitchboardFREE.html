What is VoIP?

VoIP stands for Voice over Internet Protocol and to put it simply means making telephone calls over the internet rather than
traditional telephone line. Read more about what is VoIP.

What is a VoIP Phone System?

A VoIP phone system gives you everything a traditional phone system gives you but with one crucial difference: it's hosted in the cloud.

Work smarter with a SwitchboardFREE VoIP Phone System

In 2015 BT announced their long-term plan to phase out ISDN (’normal’ phone lines), moving its services over to VoIP. The slow phase out is already underway with final-stage deactivation in 2025. As a result of deactivation in 2025, ISDN lines will no longer work therefore you will need to consider an alternative.

Benefits of a VoIP Phone System

Save money

Operating costs for VoIP phone systems are significantly lower than traditional phone systems.

Management features

Call redirection; call queueing; adding departments; missed call and voicemail services; reporting and statistical capabilities.

Future-proof

Replacing your business telephone system with a VoIP phone system, you can be sure that your telephony is modern and doesn't risk becoming redundant.

Stable and reliable

VoIP telephone systems are stable and reliable. Cloud storage also means you can save important calls and voicemails forever.

Accessibility

Wherever you are in the world you can receive your business calls and manage them effectively.

Disaster Recovery

Warehouse flooded? Office on fire? Can’t get to work? Work from anywhere and business can continue as usual.

Portability

Using a VoIP network means you can enable mobile users as well as desk-based users to connect to your telephone system.

Easy integration

VoIP phone systems can be easily integrated with your current systems for databasing, diary management, recording and reporting statistics.

Increased productivity

Take business calls anywhere so that you can work whilst or instead of traveling; call outcomes can be linked to your databases/systems and call features to enhance your service.

High call quality

Because VoIP uses your internet connection, it offers higher call quality when compared to alternatives.

Scalability

VoIP systems allow you to add a line as you hire a new employee and remove lines in the case of downsizing. You only ever pay for what you need.

Tech support

Some VoIP phone system companies offer free after-sales support. Many service providers are smaller UK businesses offering more personal and dependable support.

Choice of hardware/software

You can have a deskphone; a DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) phone (cordless/wireless phone), have a ‘softphone’ on your laptop, use your mobile phone or a combination of any of the above!
